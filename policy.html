Dreamliner Booking Policy

Smooth Journeys, Superior Comfort

1. Female Passenger Safety Policy

To ensure the safety and comfort of our female travelers, Dreamliner enforces a strict "Adjacent Seat" protocol:

Mandatory Rule: A seat directly adjacent (horizontally) to a seat already booked by a female passenger cannot be booked by a male passenger from a separate booking transaction.
  • Automated Restriction: Our system automatically blocks male gender selection for seats neighboring a female passenger.
  • Co-Traveler Exception: This restriction does not apply if both seats are being booked within the same transaction (e.g., a family or couple booking together).
  • Operator Authority: The Bus Operator reserves the right to reassign seats if it is found that a male passenger has intentionally bypassed this safety feature by providing incorrect gender information.

2. Booking & Cancellation

  • Reschedule available up to 12 hours before departure. One-time limit applies.
  • Digital or M-Ticket is valid for boarding.
  • Reporting time: 15 minutes prior.
  • Tickets are non-transferable and valid only for the trip date and time specified.
  • Passengers must carry a valid ID (Aadhaar, Voter ID, etc.) that matches the name on the e-ticket.
  • Cancellations made 24 hours before departure are eligible for a partial refund as per standard slab rates.

3. GST & Service Fees

As per government regulations, a 18% GST is applicable on all luxury bus transport services. This is calculated on the base fare and shown clearly during the checkout process.
